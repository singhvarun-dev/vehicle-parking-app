Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a parking spot?
Login, go to your dashboard, select a parking lot, vehicle type, duration and book automatically available spot.
Can I cancel my booking?
You can release (vacate) your parking spot anytime from your dashboard.
What happens after booking?
You'll get a booking QR code to show on arrival.
How is parking cost calculated?
Cost is calculated per hour, rounded up to the next hour, multiplied by lot price.
